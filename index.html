How It Works

First, you open an app on a phone or tablet. You can see lots of restaurants and their yummy food pictures. You pick what you want to eat from the menu.

  • 01 Select Restaurant

    When you decide what you want, the app sends a message to the restaurant. It's like telling them, 'Hey, I want this food, please!'

  • 02 Select menu

    Users select the restaurant and items they want to order from the available menu. They can customize their order, add special instructions, and choose the quantity.

  • 03 Wait for delivery

    The delivery partner arrives at the restaurant, picks up the order, and verifies the items to ensure accuracy and he reaches the user's location. Users can track the order status and estimated delivery time through the app.
